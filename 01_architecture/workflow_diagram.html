üîÑ Feedback Loop Mechanism

The system can iterate up to 3 times (configurable). On each iteration:

  • Iteration 1: Broad exploratory research across all planned topics
  • Iteration 2: Targeted research to address gaps identified by Analyst
  • Iteration 3: Final refinement with strict finalization criteria

This adaptive approach balances quality with cost/time efficiency, stopping early when quality is sufficient.
